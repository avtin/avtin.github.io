About Harlington

Since 1974, our management companies have been renting homes to New Yorkers looking for quality apartments at competitive prices. Our family-oriented approach to ownership and management allows us to treat each resident with a practiced level of courtesy and assistance.

Our goal is to make you feel at home. Whether you are searching for a pet-friendly NYC building, a no fee apartment (when you rent directly from us) or a home suitable for your family, roommate or just yourself, we offer a space for every need. With residential buildings in Manhattan, Queens and The Bronx, we offer a diverse portfolio comprised of more than fifty properties and nearly one thousand apartments.

In addition, we own and manage more than one hundred retail and professional spaces, and our approach boasts the same level of consideration and attention. Retail locations are primarily on Long Island, as well as Manhattan, The Bronx, and Queens. Our premiere storage facility, Temple Terrace Business and Storage Park, is located in Tampa Bay, Florida.

Based in New York City, our team is a tight-nit group of dedicated professionals ready to help our residents and retail tenants have the best possible renting experience.

Our management companies include:

  • Harlington Realty Co. LLC
  • Clearwater Properties LLC
  • Flagg Equities LLC
  • North West LLC
  • TTS Park Associates
 

Our team handles the day-to-day operations for all buildings, regardless of the associated management company.
